Rishabh Tripathi

28 July, 2024

I'm a Product Designer based in Bangalore, India. Originally from Lucknow, I moved to Bangalore to study Philosophy at Azim Premji University. I cleared my A levels with a focus on Commerce, Mathematics, and Computer Science.

During my A-levels, I developed a fascination for value creation, inspired by Varun Mayya. This inspiration has led me to become a keen observer of how people, NGOs, and businesses contribute to society and the economy. I'm constantly amazed by the ways in which technology and design impact lives on a global scale.

Since I first got my hands on my father's office laptop, I've been captivated by the process of building and designing. There's something profound about seeing the results of labor and creativity, whether it's a video I've edited or a design I've crafted. This feeling is one of my biggest motivations to wake up every day.

I believe I have a natural ability to understand and analyze experiences. This draws me towards the craftsmanship of product design. After all, we all live for experiences, so why not work on things that enhance them?

Thank you for hopping on to my corner of the internet. I have crafted it with immense love and direction to show myself. I hope you find something interesting here.
